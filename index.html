What is NexBuildr

Built for the next generation of entrepreneurs

NexBuildr is a community platform where students and young entrepreneurs connect, collaborate, and launch their ideas into the world. From your first idea to your first product — we are with you.

Our mission is to lower the barrier for young builders in India and help them find co-founders, mentors, and early users — all in one place.

For Founders

Showcase your startup, get feedback, and connect with potential co-founders.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is NexBuildr?
NexBuildr is a community platform for students and young entrepreneurs in India. It helps you connect with other builders, showcase your startup, share ideas on the feed, and collaborate through chat and AI tools.
Who can use NexBuildr?
NexBuildr is built for students, young entrepreneurs, developers, designers, and anyone who wants to build something meaningful. Whether you have an idea or a fully launched startup, there is a place for you here.
Is NexBuildr free to use?
Yes. The Free plan gives you access to the feed, global chat, one startup listing, and a basic profile — completely free, forever. Pro and Premium plans unlock additional features like Nex AI, personal chat, and more.
How does the Startup Showcase work?
You can list your startup with a name, description, logo, and website link. Other builders can browse and click your listing, increasing your view count. Featured startups get highlighted placement across the platform.
Is Nex AI available on the free plan?
Nex AI is available on the Pro and Premium plans. It helps you refine your startup pitch, brainstorm ideas, get feedback on your product, and much more. The API integration is currently in progress.
