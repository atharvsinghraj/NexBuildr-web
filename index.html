FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is NexBuildr?
NexBuildr is a community platform for students and young entrepreneurs in India. Connect with builders, showcase your startup, share ideas, and collaborate through chat and AI tools.
Who can use NexBuildr?
NexBuildr is for students, young entrepreneurs, developers, designers, and anyone who wants to build something meaningful. Whether you have an idea or a fully launched startup, there is a place for you here.
Is NexBuildr free?
Yes. The Free plan gives you access to the feed, global chat, one startup listing, and a basic profile — completely free, forever.
How does the Startup Showcase work?
List your startup with a name, description, stage, and website. Builders can browse and view your listing — startups are ranked by their view count. The Explore page is public so anyone can discover your startup.
Is Nex AI available on the free plan?
Nex AI is available on Pro and Premium plans. The API integration is currently in progress and will be available soon.
