What is NexBuildr?

A platform built for the next generation of builders

NexBuildr is more than a social network — it's a launchpad. We built it specifically for students and young entrepreneurs who have ideas but need a community to refine, validate, and grow them.

Whether you're building your first MVP or looking for co-founders, NexBuildr gives you the tools, the network, and the AI support to move fast.

🎓
For Students
Turn classroom ideas into real ventures. Connect with like-minded peers across campuses and disciplines.
🚀
For Young Founders
Showcase your startup, find early users, get feedback, and attract collaborators — all in one place.
🤖
AI-Powered Support
Nex AI helps you validate ideas, draft pitches, and navigate the early stages of building a startup.
🌐
Our Mission
Democratize entrepreneurship. Make it possible for anyone, anywhere, to build something meaningful.

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before joining the NexBuildr community.

NexBuildr is a community platform built specifically for students and young entrepreneurs. It combines social networking, startup tools, and AI assistance into one platform to help the next generation of builders share ideas, find collaborators, and grow their startups.
NexBuildr is designed for students, young founders, aspiring entrepreneurs, developers, designers, and anyone who has an idea and wants to build something. Whether you're in college, just out, or working on your first side project — you belong here.
Yes! The Basic plan is completely free — no credit card required. You get access to the Feed, profile, global chat, and one startup listing. Pro and Premium plans unlock advanced features like full Nex AI access, personal DMs, and more.
NexBuildr helps startups through the Startup Showcase (get visibility and early users), community feedback on the Feed, AI-assisted ideation and pitch writing via Nex AI, and the ability to find co-founders and collaborators through chat and profiles.
Nex AI is available in limited capacity on the Basic plan. Full, unrestricted access to Nex AI — including advanced features and a dedicated AI model — is available on Pro and Premium plans respectively.
